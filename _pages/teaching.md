<section id="philosophy" class="mb-5">
  <h2 class="h5 font-weight-bold mb-4 text-primary text-uppercase"><i class="fas fa-university mr-2"></i> Teaching Philosophy</h2>
  <p class="text-justify">
    Since 2020, I have been teaching optimization, artificial intelligence, and engineering modeling, primarily at <strong>ISAE-SUPAERO</strong> and <strong>Université Toulouse Capitole</strong>. My pedagogy emphasizes <strong>learning by doing</strong>: I bridge mathematical foundations with hands-on labs and concrete projects, with a strong focus on reproducibility and modern software engineering practices (Python, Jupyter, Git).
  </p>
  <p class="text-justify small text-secondary">
    My objective is to foster scientific autonomy and critical thinking, specifically by guiding students through the fundamental trade-offs between <strong>performance, explainability, and algorithmic complexity</strong>.
  </p>
</section>
