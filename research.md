<!-- Core Research Contributions -->
<section id="contributions" class="mb-5">
  <h2 class="h5 font-weight-bold mb-4 text-primary text-uppercase"><i class="fas fa-microscope mr-2"></i> Core Research Contributions</h2>
  
  <div class="contribution-item mb-5">
    <h3 class="h6 font-weight-bold text-dark mb-2">1. Frugal AI and High-Dimensional Surrogate Modeling</h3>
    <p class="small text-justify text-secondary">
      As the lead developer of the <strong>Surrogate Modeling Toolbox (SMT 2.0)</strong>, I develop kernels for hierarchical design variables and mixed-integer spaces. These methods enable the optimization of complex architectures by significantly reducing the required number of high-fidelity simulation calls.
    </p>
    <div class="mt-2">
      <a href="https://github.com/SMTorg/smt" target="_blank" class="badge badge-dark p-2 mr-2 shadow-sm"><i class="fab fa-github mr-1"></i> SMT 2.0 Repository</a>
    </div>
  </div>

  <div class="contribution-item mb-5">
    <h3 class="h6 font-weight-bold text-dark mb-2">2. Explainable AI (XAI) for Complex Engineering</h3>
    <p class="small text-justify text-secondary">
      Through the <strong>SMT-Explainability</strong> framework, I integrate Shapley values and global sensitivity analysis into surrogate models. This allows researchers to move beyond "black-box" optimization, providing physical insights into model behavior for robust decision support.
    </p>
  </div>

  <div class="contribution-item mb-5">
    <h3 class="h6 font-weight-bold text-dark mb-2">3. SEGOMOE: Mixture of Experts for Global Optimization</h3>
    <p class="small text-justify text-secondary">
      My work on <strong>SEGOMOE</strong> (Super Efficient Global Optimization with Mixture of Experts) focuses on Bayesian optimization frameworks that handle complex mixed-variable constraints, particularly for large-scale aerospace engineering applications.
    </p>
  </div>
</section>
